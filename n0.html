Faker Proves why he is the Undisputed Champion in Worlds 2024

"He's still the GOAT."

Article published by: @jessepinkman17

Credit: Riot Games

Faker, one of the most well known players in the League of Legends, has shown the world once again why he is one of the, if not, the best player in the game with his spectacular performance in the recently concluded League tournament Worlds 2024.

Together with his formidable teammates, Zeus, Oner, Keria, and Gumayusi, they were able to qualify for the tournaments and dominated every single one of their opponents all the way to the finals, when they faced off against Bilibili Gaming.

Last year, it was cakewalk for T1 against Bilibili Gaming. However, they definitely stepped up their game this year. It was a best-of-five game where both teams were tied 2-2 in the first four rounds.

In the final decisive match, everyone was pushing their limits and pulling off the most intense performances the tournament has seen so far. It got the audience riled up and cheering crazily for their teams.

Alas, when it mattered the most, Faker stepped up and pushed ahead of everyone else, ultimately securing T1's victory and earned the MVP title, also cementing his status as the 'Greatest of All Time'. Faker says he grateful for his team that they were able to come this far as they have won in Worlds two consecutive times now.
