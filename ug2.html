DOOM: The Dark Ages

Action, Shooter

Article published by: @gusfring50

Credit: id Software

DOOM: The Dark Ages, is an upcoming first-person shooter developed by id Software, and is the successor to DOOM: Eternal (2020).

The story is set during a time before the main events of DOOM, so it serves as a prequel to the original series.

Play as Doom Slayer and mow down countless enemies in your way while you progress through the thrilling story! With brand new content, improved graphics and a new soundtrack album, this game is well-deserved of its hype.

The game supports PC and console platforms, is available upon purchase and is set to release in the year 2025, date not confirmed yet. So stay tuned for more updates and potential discounts!
