Top 5 Best Characters to Dominate Your Opponents in TEKKEN 8

"The road to the King (or Queen) of the Iron Fist Tournament is not an easy path."

Article published by: @walterwhite52

Credit: Tekken Project

Character Strengths in Tekken

All of the characters throughout the Tekken games have been seen as well-balanced, with no major powercreepings and such, just purely skills, timings, and counters. With that said, here are 5 of the best characters in Tekken 8 that not only beginners can master easily, but veteran players as well.

1. Jin

Credit: BANDAI Namco

Kazama Jin, a strong martial artist, is more than just the poster boy of the Tekken games series. His skillset has been revamped to the point where it is easy to tell and time your combos. He is also beginner-friendly, as he is very easy to master, while still being able to overwhelm opponents when played right.

2. Claudio

Credit: BANDAI Namco

Just like Jin, Claudio Serafino is also easy to learn and taking the time to learn his moveset can greatly benefit you as you can possibly use him to his fullest potential. He doesn't have long and learny combo patterns, instead he has various abilities that are strong enough to disrupt your opponents' flow.

3. Hwoarang

Credit: BANDAI Namco

Hwoarang is a strong martial artist who focuses more on his kicks. Due to having a wide range of kicking techniques, Hwoarang can be very dangerous to go against especially if the player takes the time to master his attack patterns.

In addition to that, in Heat mode, he is able to utilise more tools in order to counter his opponents' defenses, allowing Hwoarang to perform more combos and deal greater damage.

4. Jack-8

Credit: BANDAI Namco

Jack-8 is one of the game's largest characters with very high offensive capabilities as well as a poking playstyle. He may be slow in mobility, but watch out, because if opponents get too close, they may see their health bar depleting super fast from Jack-8's explosive power.

Players who use Jack-8 should be wary of opponents with high mobility, as they can bait you in performing wrong moves and leaving them an opening to attack.

5. Alisa

Credit: BANDAI Namco

Alisa Bosconovitch is a great pick for players who want to use characters with a fun playstyle with high mobility. Her speed allows players to dance around the battlefield whilst waiting for the perfect opportunity to deliver counterattacks once the opponent initiates.
