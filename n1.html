Fortnite Chapter 1 is Coming Back Soon - Good News for Veteran Players!

"A dream come true."

Article published by: @jessepinkman17

Credit: Epic Games

Today is a great day for those who have been playing Fortnite since it was released. We all knew about the controversial moment many years ago when Epic Games decided to remove its beloved Chapter One from the game, enraging fans.

However, there have been major leaks about Chapter One coming back during December as a permanent gamemode which shocked the playerbase. It spread all across the internet, even YouTubers and platforms like Twitter are voicing their thoughts about this comeback.

Chapter One contained a map which veteran players loved, so expect new maps, guns, skins, and returning players as well. Newer players who haven't experienced the old days of Fortnite wouldn't want to miss this!
