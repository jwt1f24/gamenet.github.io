5 Strongest Builds for Elden Ring - Overpower Your Enemies!

"Become the next Elden Lord."

Article published by: @walterwhite52

Credit: FromSoftware Inc.

Builds in Elden Ring

In Elden Ring, your character's builds play an important role in clearing content. And as many updates went by, lots of builds that used to be strong have now been rendered pretty much useless. However, this didn't stop players from coming up with new and creative builds. So here are 5 of the current best builds to help players have an easier time with the difficult challenges of Elden Ring.

1. Berserker

Taking inspiration from the popular manga character Guts, this build focuses purely on strength, equipped with a gigantic greatsword and the immense damage that can be dealt with this build.

From being able to take on multiple targets to even the strongest and most difficult bosses, this build has simply so much defensive and offensive stats that it can make you borderline unstoppable.


2. Moon Wizard

This build can be paired with a greatsword, which enables the player to fight both in close range combat and also shoot ranged projectiles. By using this build effectively, players can send out strong projectiles through heavy attacks.


3. Magma Manipulator

Between lighting-based and fire-based builds, players usually prefer the former due to their destructivity. However, this fire-based is unlike any other. It is centered around the mechanic of rapid firing various abilities.

No matter how strong the enemy is, the right enhancements are capable of dispatching them swiftly. This makes it one of best Pyromancy builds in the game.


4. Star Beam Wizard

This wizardry setup focuses on shooting strong laser beams. You may ask yourself, what makes a build about laser beams so popular? When equipped with the right buffs against bosses, you can potentially take them out in one hit!


5. Blood King

Bleeding is an effective combat technique, and when this build is paired with shields and enhancements, the player's offensive and defensive stats will increase, ensuring better survivability rate against bosses.

The downside to this build though, is that it is weak against enemies who are immune to bleeding, so it may not be a good idea to use this build everytime.
