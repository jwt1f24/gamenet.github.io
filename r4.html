Elden Ring is One of the Best Games of All Time - Here's Why

"This game is peak."

Article published by: @saulgoodman12

Credit: FromSoftware Inc.

Elden Ring is a very popular game and it is universally praised by the internet. I picked up this game the other day as I was interested in it. And I got to say, this game absolutely blew my mind.

After completing most of the story, it is without a doubt a masterpiece. The story, details, gameplay, soundtrack, every aspect of the game is just out of this world. I cannot think of anything negative to say about this game because it is just THAT good.

In this game, there is more freedom of choice. You get to do whatever you want in the open-world while still pursuing the goal of becoming the Elden Lord. The game is also challenging, its enemies are strong and it forces you to learn the game's mechanics and improve in combat skills. These kind of games are my cup of tea.

Not to mention, the artstyle and dialogue options are so realistic, it was as if I used a time machine to travel back to medieval era.

Without a doubt, a 10/10 game. One of the most fascinating games I have ever played.
