GTA VI

Action, Adventure

Article published by: @gusfring50

Credit: Rockstar Games

Grand Theft Auto VI is the latest installment of the GTA series, and is the successor to Grand Theft Auto V (2013) developed by Rockstar Games.

Like its predecessors, the game will be about action, crime, and adventure. However, this time around, the developers have taken their graphic design, attention to detail, soundtrack quality, and many other the game's aspects to the next level.

The game focuses on the two main characters, Jason and Lucia, as they explore the wonders and dangers of Miami's Vice City, the iconic city of nightlife, while indulging in crime and heists that makes the police go on a crazy manhunt! Fans who grew up with the GTA games are no doubt getting this game as soon as it is released.

This game also contains 18+ content, so parental guidance is advised.

The game supports only console platforms, is available upon purchase and is set to release on the fall season of 2025, which would be around late 2025. So stay tuned for more updates and potential discounts!
