3 Best Agents to Rank Up Fast in Low Elo

"Strong yet easy to master."

Article published by: @walterwhite52

Credit: Riot Games

Struggling in Low Elo

In the competitive game of VALORANT, players often struggle in the lower ranks such as Iron, Bronze, Silver, and Gold. This is usually caused by weak mastery in the agent the player is playing as. In this case, we have carefully selected 3 of the most beginner-friendly but effective agents in the game, both offensive and defensive, to help ranking up easier.

1. Chamber

Credit: Riot Games

Chamber is easily one the best agents in all of VALORANT due to his arsenal of lethal weapons and his ability to dispatch opponents and swiftly leave the scene almost unscathed.

Players who prefer playing in a more tactical fashion will not be dissapointed with Chamber's performance and charming personality.

2. Brimstone

Credit: Riot Games

Brimstone is a popular pick among the lower ranks because not only is he a free agent, but he is also very easy to play. Brimstone's main strength comes from his ability to cover areas with smokes to assist teammates in battle. Not only that, his ultimate can be a game changer when used correctly.

Players who prefer a defensive role will have an enjoyable experience with Brimstone.

3. Cypher

Credit: Riot Games

Cypher is a comfort agent of many due to the fact that he has more of a sneaky playstyle compared to other agents. His ability to set up tripwires, hidden cameras, and revealing enemy positions makes him a deadly opponent to fight against when played cautiously.

Players who enjoy laid-back playstyles and setting up traps will love playing as Cypher.
