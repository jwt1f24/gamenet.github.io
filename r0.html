This Card Trading Simulator has been Going Viral Recently - Let's Check it Out!

"An addictive and unique simulator game"

Article published by: @saulgoodman12

Credit: OPNeon Games

Recently, there's this game called 'TCG Card Shop Simulator' that has been blowing up. It appears like everyone all over internet is talking about this game, even my friends and several content creators I watch. I thought to myself: "Why not give this game a try? Surely it is popular for a good reason, right?" So I tried it, and here are my thoughts on this game.

So we play as the store owner and start off our work shift by greeting customers and attending to their purchases. There is also an area in which customers can sit down and play matches and trade with others. The funniest part to me though, is that we can spray a bottle of deodorant to customers who may appear unpleasant. Talk about stereotypes of TCG players not showering!

This is definitely a comfort game I can play when I'm free and trying to kill time. All in all, I would give this game an 8/10 rating. The game was just released recently, so I'm sure there is definitely room for more potential.
