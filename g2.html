Top 5 Strongest Junglers to Rank Up Faster in League

"Become the next legendary jungler."

Article published by: @walterwhite52

Credit: Riot Games

The Importance of Jungling

In League of Legends, players get to choose heroes of different roles, with junglers being one the most popular ones. However, junglers are not easy master, and players have to fully understand the mechanics of the character they are playing as.


With the recent update, some heroes have gotten nerfs, while some others have gotten buffs. So, we have listed down the 5 best junglers that is sure to win you more fights and matches.

1. Kasiks

Kasiks is an assassin who specializes in fighting lone opponents and dealing high single-target burst-damage. What makes him unique is that he is able to adapt to different situations throughout the match due to his varied abilities.

2. Morgan

Morgan is a witch who is strong in controlling the battefield whilst reducing damage intake. Her useful stunning technique allows her to be played both offensively and defensively. This makes her a really good pick for jungling.

3. Nocturna

Nocturna is an assassin who can dish out high burst damage while catching enemies off-guard. His high mobility, unpredictable playstyle, and ultimate, which allows him to reduce enemies' map vision, makes him one of the deadliest junglers to play against.

4. Amu

Amu is a tank who focuses on engaging in team fights. His ability to initiate team fights and control multiple enemies at once makes him a great tank for jungling.

5. Ollybear

Ollybear is a fighter who excels in disrupting enemy team formation. He can counter enemy turrets, which makes him strong for turret diving and securing kills. His durability and abilities make him a strong jungler especially in team fights.
