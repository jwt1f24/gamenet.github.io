Minecraft is a Game Like No Other

"Forever one of the best games ever created."

Article published by: @saulgoodman12

Credit: Mojang AB

I have never played Minecraft during my childhood, as it was inaccessible for me back then. But recently, I saved up some money and finally got to try it out for the first time ever. So here my opinions on this game.

We play as Steve (or Alex), in a world filled with endless mysteries. We start out by gathering resources, surviving from monsters throught the nights, gather rare ores from mining.

I thought: "This isn't that hard." And boy oh boy was I proven wrong. To progress further into the game, we have to explore the Nether, a hellscape with an unforgiving environment. My time with the Nether was so challenging, but it was still fun. Afterwards, the finale of my journey was to face the Ender Dragon in the End. After a couple of devastating fails, I finally defeated the Ender Dragon and reacched the end of the story.

But that wasn't the end of the game, as in reality the game is only limited by out imagination. There was creative mode, where we can spend time building our own creations without any limitations. And there was also hardcore mode, taking survival to the extreme, you only live once and once you die, it's game over.

The soundtrack is also a big contributor to the game's atmosphere. From the unsettling sounds in caves, to the calming music during sunrise and evening, the composers definitely did a great job.

This game is without a doubt my new favourite game with a 10/10 rating. Nowadays I always make time just to play this game because it is so therapeutic.
