Honest Thoughts on Celeste

"A one-of-a-kind platformer game."

Article published by: @saulgoodman12

Credit: Maddy Makes Games

Celeste has always been a game I have heard of but never got around to playing it. But recently, I had a lot of free time, so I finally decided to try this game since many people I knew held this game in high regard.

So we play as Madeline, a girl in an unwell state of mind, and go on a journey while climbing Celeste Mountain. It has a lot of difficult obstacles, but yet still so engaging and addictive. Whenever I fail, I just keep coming back just to prove I can beat something this hard. In addition, the soundtrack and music in this game is so calming to listen to. It's like going on a long relaxing walk and telling myself everything will be alright.

Overall, this game is definitely one of the most fun and addictive platformer games I have ever played, and I will happily give this game a 10/10 rating, as it sort of gave me a lot of great memories while playing it.
