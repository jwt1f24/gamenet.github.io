Stardew Valley's New Update - Here's What You Need to Know

"A huge update for everyone."

Article published by: @jessepinkman17

Credit: ConcernedApe

With the upcoming hyped up new update for Stardew Valley, here's a quick breakdown of what is coming to the game soon.

Lots of new and useful items have been added:

  • Larger Chests - To store more inventory with less chests as players have been concerned with the cluttering issue in the game.
  • Enhanced Workstations - To speed up processes like ore-melting and food production, while producing in greater quantities.
  • New Accessories and Furniture - Find interesting things to decorate your farmhouse with.
  • New Secret Events - Can be found in certain times throughout the day, or seasonal events, so be prepared for time-gated rewards!

Aside from that, the developers have been taking the game in a more visual direction, by adding new terrain never seen before. This is a huge update no Stardew Valley player would want to miss out on!
