The New Call of Duty Game: First Impressions

"Is it an actually good game with potential?"

Article published by: @saulgoodman12

Credit: ActiVision

Well, if it isn't another Call of Duty Black Ops game? I am an avid fan of the Black Ops series, so this was definitely a must-try for me. However, is this game worth playing? After 20+ hours of playtime, I will share my thoughts on this game.

The campaign can be quite engaging, full of action, and it does have its moments. However, I personally feel there is still something lacking in the story, like there is nothing in the story that can make it stand out from the rest of the games.

The multiplayer gamemode on the other hand though, is so fun to play. You can choose various weapons and old and iconic operators. The graphics in this game is also over the top, like everything just flows so smoothly. The soundtracks are great as well, with interesting tones in each one.

This game definitely has it's ups and downs, so I would have to give it a rating of 6/10. I feel like if the developers could write an actual memorable story, I would totally give it at least a 8/10 rating.
