Aeroplane Simulator

Simulation

Article published by: @gusfring50

Credit: iStock

Aeroplane Simulator is what you would think it is. In this game developed by PlaneStudios, you are able to pilot an aircraft and fly all over the world!

With the use and study of geographical maps, the locations of landmarks around the world is at least 90% accurate. This gives people who are unable to travel overseas a feel of what it's like to visit other places on Earth. This simulator can also help pilots in training, as the aeroplane's physics have been adjusted to that of a real one. This allows them to practice their flying skills more.

The game supports PC and console platforms, is available upon purchase and is set to release on the winter season of 2025, which would be around early 2025. So stay tuned for more updates and potential discounts!
