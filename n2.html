New Content Coming Soon in Black Ops 6!

"You can't get enough of 'em."

Article published by: @jessepinkman17

Credit: ActiVision

Recently, the developers of Black Ops 6 have finally announced big changes, which were long awaited by the community. So let's check them out!

For Maps, we have both new ones, and old and beloved ones such as:

  • Standoff
  • Shipment
  • Monastery

Not only that, we also have interesting changes to guns:

  • (BUFF) Man-o-war now can store higher rounds and has a 5% increased fire rate
  • (NERF) M15 is getting a recoil increase
  • (ADJUSTMENT) QQ9 is getting a slight mobility reduction in exchange of having extended rounds

Some glitches and bugs that were exploited by players in certain gamemodes have been patched as well. Other than that, expect a bunch of new skins for both your characters and guns, and special holiday events!
