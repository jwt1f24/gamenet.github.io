Best Guns to for Black Ops 6 Zombies - Be Unstoppable!

"Go berserk against the zombie hordes!"

Article published by: @walterwhite52

Credit: ActiVision

Challenges of Zombies

In the game of Call of Duty, players get to choose a wide variety of weapons to combat against enemies. Unfortunately, some weapons may not be strong enough to win players fights in critical situations.


So, we will be listing several weapons we have found to be the most effective in combat compared with other weapons after hours of playtesting.

1. SVD

Credit: ActiVision

The SVD, is one of the best snipers for early game due to its fast firing rate and lethality.

Most snipers are quite lackluster when compared to other guns. However, what makes the SVD stand out the most is its ability to switch to a shotgun mode, where bullets have increased spread and damage. This makes it a very deadly in both long and short distance combat.

2. LR 7.62

Credit: ActiVision

Most snipers are underwhelming in Zombies. However, the LR 7.62 can dish out explosive damage that can eliminate multiple zombies at once, making it one the most powerful snipers in the game.

One of its downsides however, is its slow firing rate. But when played with a tactical playstyle, this gun can make you almost untouchable.

3. GS45

Credit: ActiVision

Up next, we have the GS45. We know, you may be asking yourself, why is there a handgun on this list. But don't let its appearance fool you.

The GS45 can deal ridiculously high damage, especially in close range, where enemies have almost no chance of surviving. In addition, special attachments further enhances the GS45's bullet speed.

4. PP-919

Credit: ActiVision

The PP-919 is a submachine gun that is often used in Zombies, and for good reason.

Submachine guns may not perform well compared to other guns, but the PP-919 comes with a large magazine that allows you fire more than 200 rounds. This gun is perfect for players who want to defeat multiple enemies without the need to reload frequently, allowing longer active combat time.

5. C9

Credit: ActiVision

Last but not least, we have the C9. It is a submachine with decent firing rate, mobility, and damage, which is a good choice for early game.

Its true potential is uncovered when equipped with enhancements, where both its firing rate and damage will drastically increase. Add on an extended magazine, and players can dish out rapid fire damage for longer periods of time.
